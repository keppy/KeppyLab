    About KeppyLab

    KeppyLab is an open source AI research and development laboratory focusing on biomedical data, LLMs, and adapting new techniques in AI to build more than just another chatbot. Our core guiding principals are:

    1. Open source everything you can & give users the option to use Ollama OR GPT-4 whenever possible
    2. Cross-domain pollination -- apply AI to biomedical data, apply techniques from biomedicine to AI
    3. Evidence over assumptions -- treat LLMs as black boxes and test extensively -- validate your experiments and backup claims with data

    We are in the early stages of forming the tool chain and what our core focuses will be, but there are many areas ripe for investigation.

    If you are in the Seattle area check out our monthly event, the KeppyLab AI Hackers & Paper Readers.
